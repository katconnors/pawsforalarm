FAQ


Q: What is the goal of your site?
A: To consolidate adoption posts for animals that are at risk of euthanasia.
The hope is that spreading the word to potential adopters will improve chances for the animals.
Additionally, the site boosts posts of animals that are in need of a foster home.


Q: How do you determine which animals are at risk?
A: A portion of the animals on the site have euthanasia dates, per data provided by RescueGroups.org's API.
In the case of animals in need of foster, shelter groups that enter information into the API can flag animals as needing foster.

Q: Why is an animal's listed euthanasia date in the past?
A: There are many potential answers for this.
An animal may be in foster care; if so, their date might not be updated in records.
Another explanation is that the animal is no longer available.

Q: What animals will be on this site?
A: At this time, the main animal results are of cats and dogs,
but other animal types have been supported as well.

Q: How is the site updated?
A: The information is compiled using manual requests from an API.
Ultimately, there may be more regular updates.
