FAQ


Q: What is the goal of your site?
A: To consolidate posts for animals that are at risk of euthanasia.
The hope is that spreading the word to potential adopters will improve adoption chances for the animals.

Q: How do you determine which animals are at risk?
A: The animals on the site have euthanasia dates, per data provided by RescueGroups.org's API.

Q: Why is an animal's listed euthanasia date in the past?
A: Either the animal is no longer available for adoption, or their euthanasia date has been changed.

Q: What animals will be on this site?
A: At this time, the main animals are cats and dogs.

Q: How is the site updated?
A: At this time, the information is pulled from manual API requests.
Ultimately, there may be more regular updates.
