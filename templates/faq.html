FAQ


Q: What is the goal of your site?
A: To consolidate posts for animals that are at risk of euthanasia.
Spreading the word to potential adopters could improve chances of adoption.

Q: How to do you determine which animals are at risk?
A: The animals on the site either have euthanasia dates or they appeared on urgent lists created by various shelters.

Q: Why is an animal's listed euthanasia date in the past?
A: Either the animal is no longer available for adoption, or their euthanasia date has changed.

Q: Do all animals have scheduled euthanasia dates?
A: At the time of entry, not all animals have scheduled dates.

Q: What types of animals will be on this site?
A: At this time, the main animals are cats and dogs.

Q: How is the site updated?
A: At this time, the information is updated manually by one person.
Ultimately, there may be automatic updates to the site.
